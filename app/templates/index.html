How Ubuntu Alert works

From report to resolution, every case follows the same trusted process.

1

Report

A family member, school, or police station submits details and a photo.

2

Verify

A trained team confirms identity, status, and contact details.

3

Activate

The alert is sent to watchers, partners, and social channels.

4

Resolve

Sightings are coordinated until the person is found and the case closes.
